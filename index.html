About Infinity Polymers

Infinity Polymers General Trading LLC is a global trading company specializing in Polypropylene (PP), Polyethylene (PE), PVC, and PET.

With operations spanning Europe, Asia, Canada, and Latin America, the company delivers reliable polymer supply solutions supported by international market expertise and long‑term partnerships.
